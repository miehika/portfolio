ABOUT

As a passionate and motivated professional, I’m constantly striving to improve my techniques, expand my skillset and find new opportunities to grow.

All of my projects as part of my course essentials - have provided this growth and allowed me to establish myself within this competitive industry.

I try to combine a knack of simplicity that packs a punch of analogue and abstract design skills when it comes to creating design assets, for use in social media and marketing platforms, developing and maintaining consistent branding, creating posters, translating your story into visuals, creating covers for your publications, and even creating them altogether.


Please enjoy my portfolio, and feel free to contact me. I would love to collaborate with you on your next project.
